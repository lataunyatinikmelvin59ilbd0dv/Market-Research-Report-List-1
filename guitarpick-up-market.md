<p><strong>The (Guitar)Pick-Up Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sound Hole Pickup</li><li>Piezoelectric Pickup</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The guitar pick-up market offers two main types: sound hole pick-ups and piezoelectric pick-ups. Sound hole pick-ups are placed inside the sound hole of the guitar and capture the vibrations of the strings, providing a warm and natural tone. On the other hand, piezoelectric pick-ups are installed under the bridge of the guitar and use crystals to convert the vibrations into electrical signals, resulting in a clear and bright tone. Both types cater to different preferences and playing styles in the guitar community.</p></p>
