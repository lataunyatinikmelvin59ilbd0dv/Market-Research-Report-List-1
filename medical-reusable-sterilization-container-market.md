<p><p>Medical Reusable Sterilization Containers come in two types: Filter Type and Valve Type. Filter Type containers are equipped with filters that allow for gas sterilization, ensuring effective and thorough sterilization of medical instruments. On the other hand, Valve Type containers are designed with valves that help maintain the sterility of instruments during storage and transport. Both types of containers are essential in healthcare settings to ensure the safety and integrity of medical equipment used in patient care.</p></p>
