<p><p>The Automotive Evaporative Emission Control (EVAP) System market consists of various types of components to prevent fuel vapors from escaping into the atmosphere. These components include fuel tanks, gas caps, liquid vapor separators, canisters, control valves, sensors, and hoses. The fuel tank stores the fuel and vapors, the gas cap seals the tank, the liquid vapor separator separates liquid fuel from vapors, the canister absorbs and stores vapors, the control valve regulates vapor flow, sensors monitor system performance, and hoses connect these components.</p></p>
