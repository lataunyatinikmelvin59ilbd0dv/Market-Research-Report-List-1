<p><p>Load Centers are electrical distribution boards that consist of circuit breakers to distribute power to circuits within a building. There are two main types of Load Centers based on the type of main disconnect: Main Breaker Type and Main Lugs Type. Main Breaker Type load centers have a built-in main circuit breaker for overcurrent protection, while Main Lugs Type load centers have lugs for incoming power connections but do not have a main disconnect. Both types are commonly used in residential, commercial, and industrial applications for electrical distribution.</p></p>
