<p><p>Solar control glass is a specialized type of glass used primarily in buildings to control the amount of heat and light transmitted through windows. There are two main types of solar control glass: absorbing solar control glass and reflective solar control glass. Absorbing solar control glass is dark in color and absorbs solar radiation, reducing the amount of heat transmitted into a building. Reflective solar control glass, on the other hand, has a metallic coating that reflects solar radiation, minimizing heat gain and glare. Both types help improve energy efficiency and comfort within buildings.</p></p>
