<p><ul><li>Hard Tag</li><li>Soft Tag</li><li>Deactivator or Detacher</li><li>Detection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>EAS Systems market consists of various types of products used for theft prevention in retail stores. Hard tags are durable and reusable attached to items, while soft tags are disposable and easily removable. Deactivators or detachers are used to remove tags at the point of sale. Detection systems are installed at store exits to sound an alarm if a tagged item is taken without deactivation. These products work together to prevent shoplifting and protect merchandise.</p></p>
