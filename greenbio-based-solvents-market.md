<p><p>Green/bio-based solvents are eco-friendly alternatives to traditional solvents derived from petroleum. These solvents can be categorized into various types, including esters, alcohols, D-Limonene, and others. Esters are derived from natural sources such as plants and fruits, while alcohols are typically derived from sugar feedstocks. D-Limonene is a terpene-based solvent extracted from citrus fruits. Other types of green solvents may include glycols, lactate esters, and others derived from biomass. These solvents are gaining popularity due to their environmental benefits and low toxicity levels.</p></p>
