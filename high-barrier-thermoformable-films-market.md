<p><p>High barrier thermoformable films are available in various types such as PVC, PCTFE, PE, and PP. PVC films offer good barrier properties but are not suitable for high-temperature applications. PCTFE films provide superior barrier performance against moisture, gases, and aromas. PE films are cost-effective and flexible, making them suitable for a wide range of applications. PP films offer excellent chemical resistance and moisture barrier properties, making them ideal for food packaging. Each type of film caters to specific requirements in various industries.</p></p>
