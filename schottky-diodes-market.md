<p><p>Schottky diodes are available in two main market types - Through Hole Technology and Surface Mount Technology. Through Hole Technology involves diodes being soldered into holes on a circuit board, providing a strong mechanical connection. Surface Mount Technology, on the other hand, involves diodes being soldered onto the surface of the board, saving space and allowing for automated assembly. Both types offer various benefits and are widely used in electronic devices for applications requiring fast switching speeds and low forward voltage drop.</p></p>
