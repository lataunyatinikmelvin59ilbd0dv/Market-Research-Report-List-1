<p><p>Construction dumper market is classified into three types: Articulated Dump Trucks, Rigid Dump Trucks, and Others. Articulated Dump Trucks feature a hinge between the cab and the dump box, allowing for better maneuverability on rough terrains. Rigid Dump Trucks have fixed frames and are suitable for larger-scale construction projects. The Others category includes variations like mini dumpers, tracked dumpers, and dump trailers. Each type offers unique features to cater to different construction needs.</p></p>
