<p><p>Membrane touch switch (MTS) comes in two main types: with rubber dome and without rubber dome. The type with a rubber dome features a layer of flexible rubber that provides tactile feedback when pressed. On the other hand, the type without a rubber dome uses a flat membrane over a printed circuit board. Both types offer durable and reliable switch functionality, with the choice between them depending on the desired tactile feel and design requirements.</p></p>
