<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Injection Epoxy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Viscosity</li><li>Super Low Viscosity</li><li>Medium Viscosity</li><li>High Viscosity</li><li>Extra High Viscosity</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Injection epoxy market types are classified based on their viscosity levels. Low viscosity epoxy is ideal for penetrating deep into cracks and crevices. Super low viscosity epoxy is designed for quick and easy application in tight spaces. Medium viscosity epoxy offers a balance between flowability and bonding strength. High viscosity epoxy is suitable for vertical surfaces and overhead applications. Extra high viscosity epoxy provides maximum bonding strength and is commonly used for structural repairs and heavy-duty applications.</p></p>
