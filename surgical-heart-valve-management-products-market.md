<p><p>Surgical heart valve management products market includes various types such as percutaneous balloon valvuloplasty, heart valve surgery, heart valve repair, and heart valve replacement. Percutaneous balloon valvuloplasty is a minimally invasive procedure to widen narrowed heart valves. Heart valve surgery involves open-heart procedures to repair or replace damaged valves. Heart valve repair focuses on preserving the natural valve structure, while heart valve replacement involves replacing the valve with a mechanical or biological prosthesis. These products aim to improve heart function and overall quality of life for patients.</p></p>
