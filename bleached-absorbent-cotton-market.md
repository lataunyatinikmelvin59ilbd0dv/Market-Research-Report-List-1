<p><p>Virgin cotton refers to pure and untreated cotton that has not been processed or mixed with any other fibers. It is known for its high quality and absorbency, making it a popular choice for medical and hygiene products. Noil cotton, on the other hand, is a byproduct of the cotton spinning process and is considered lower in quality compared to virgin cotton. Noil cotton is often used in lower-end products like nonwoven fabrics and upholstery padding.</p></p>
