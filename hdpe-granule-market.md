<p><ul><li>Autoclave Process</li><li>Tubular Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HDPE Granule market types refer to the different manufacturing processes used to produce high-density polyethylene granules. Autoclave Process involves using high pressure and temperature to polymerize ethylene gas, resulting in a higher quality product suitable for various applications. Tubular Process, on the other hand, uses a continuous extrusion process to produce granules with consistent quality and properties. Both processes have their advantages and are used in different industries depending on the specific requirements of the end products.</p></p>
