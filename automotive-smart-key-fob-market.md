<p><ul><li>Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES)</li><li>Remote Keyless Entry System (RKES</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Smart Key Fob market comprises two major types: Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry Systems (RKES). PKES is a convenient technology that automatically unlocks the vehicle door when the key fob is in close proximity, allowing for easy entry without manually pressing any buttons. On the other hand, RKES requires the user to press a button on the key fob to lock or unlock the vehicle remotely. Both technologies provide enhanced security and convenience to car owners.</p></p>
