<p><p>The irrigation automation system market can be classified into automatic and semi-automatic types. Automatic systems operate independently, utilizing sensors and timers to automatically adjust watering schedules based on environmental conditions. These systems require minimal human intervention and are preferred for large-scale agricultural operations due to their efficiency and convenience. On the other hand, semi-automatic systems involve some level of manual control, allowing users to adjust settings based on their specific needs. These systems are suitable for smaller agricultural fields or gardens where more customized watering schedules are desired.</p></p>
