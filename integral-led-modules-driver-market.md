<p><p>Integral LED modules are available in two types of drivers - constant current driver and constant voltage driver. Constant current drivers supply a consistent current to the LED module, ensuring stable and reliable performance. This type of driver is commonly used in applications where variations in voltage may occur. Constant voltage drivers provide a consistent voltage to the LED module, making them suitable for applications where a fixed voltage is required. Both types of drivers play a crucial role in ensuring the proper functioning of LED modules.</p></p>
