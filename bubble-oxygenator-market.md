<p><strong>The Bubble Oxygenator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hard Shell Oxygenator</li><li>Bag Type Oxygenator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bubble Oxygenator market comprises two main types: Hard Shell Oxygenators and Bag Type Oxygenators. Hard Shell Oxygenators are enclosed in a rigid housing and use a rotating disc to create bubbles, allowing for efficient oxygen transfer. This type is known for its durability and ease of use. On the other hand, Bag Type Oxygenators utilize flexible bags to generate bubbles, improving gas exchange. They offer greater adaptability and are commonly used in perfusion procedures during cardiac surgeries. Both types are essential in providing oxygenation support to patients undergoing cardiac procedures.</p></p>
