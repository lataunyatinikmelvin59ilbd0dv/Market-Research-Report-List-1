<p><p>Industrial rubber repair adhesives are divided into two main types: hot bond and cold bond. Hot bond adhesives require heat to activate and bond the rubber components together, offering a strong and enduring repair solution. Cold bond adhesives, on the other hand, do not require heat and are applied at room temperature, making them a more convenient option for certain repair applications. Both types of adhesives offer effective solutions for repairing industrial rubber components, catering to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
