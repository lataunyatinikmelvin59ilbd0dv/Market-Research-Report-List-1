<p><p>MTP connectors can be categorized into two main market types: male connectors and female connectors. Male connectors feature projecting pins that fit into corresponding holes on female connectors, allowing for a secure and reliable connection. Female connectors, on the other hand, have receptacles to accept the pins from male connectors. Both types play a crucial role in various industries and applications where high-performance and efficient fiber optic connections are required.</p></p>
