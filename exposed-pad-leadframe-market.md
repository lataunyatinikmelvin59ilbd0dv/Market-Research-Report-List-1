<p><p>The Exposed Pad Leadframe market consists of two main types: Stamping Process Lead Frame and Etching Process Lead Frame. Stamping Process Lead Frames are manufactured by stamping the lead frame material to create the desired shape and design. On the other hand, Etching Process Lead Frames are produced by selectively removing material using chemical etching techniques. Both types of lead frames are commonly used in the semiconductor industry for packaging integrated circuits and electronic components, offering various benefits such as cost-effectiveness, flexibility, and high precision.</p></p>
