<p><p>Ship-to-Shore Container Cranes are classified into two main market types: Single-container Ship-to-Shore Container Cranes and Double-container Ship-to-Shore Container Cranes. Single-container cranes are designed to handle one container at a time, whereas double-container cranes have the capacity to lift and move two containers simultaneously. Single-container cranes are more commonly used in smaller ports with lower volumes of cargo, while double-container cranes are efficient in larger ports with higher container traffic. Both types play a crucial role in the efficient handling of containers at port terminals.</p></p>
