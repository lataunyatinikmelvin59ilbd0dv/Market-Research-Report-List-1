<p><p>Coated fertilizers are granular fertilizers that have been coated with materials such as sulfur, polymers, or other compounds to modify their release properties. These coatings help control the release of nutrients, reducing nutrient leaching and improving plant uptake efficiency. Coated fertilizers are becoming increasingly popular due to their ability to enhance crop yields while minimizing environmental impact.</p><p>The coated fertilizers market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 0.67% during the forecast period. The growth is driven by increasing demand for high-efficiency fertilizers that can improve crop productivity and sustainability. Farmers are increasingly adopting coated fertilizers to optimize nutrient use and reduce fertilizer wastage. Additionally, there is a growing focus on environmentally friendly agricultural practices, further boosting the demand for coated fertilizers.</p><p>The latest trends in the coated fertilizers market include the development of innovative coating technologies that improve nutrient release efficiency and reduce nutrient losses. Manufacturers are investing in research and development to create customized coated fertilizers that meet specific crop and soil requirements. Furthermore, the adoption of precision agriculture techniques is driving the demand for coated fertilizers as they help in targeted nutrient delivery, leading to improved crop yields.</p></p>
