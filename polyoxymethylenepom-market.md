<p><p>Polyoxymethylene(POM) is a versatile engineering thermoplastic derived from formaldehyde. It is available in two main types - homo-polyoxymethylene and co-polyoxymethylene. Homo-polyoxymethylene, also known as homopolymer POM, is a type of POM composed of repeating units of the same monomer. On the other hand, co-polyoxymethylene, also known as copolymer POM, is a type of POM composed of repeating units of two different monomers. Both types have their own unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
