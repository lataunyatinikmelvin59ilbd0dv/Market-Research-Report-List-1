<p><ul><li>Stand-Alone Residential Air Purifiers</li><li>In-Duct Residential Air Purifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HEPA-Based Residential Air Purifiers come in two main types: Stand-Alone and In-Duct models. Stand-Alone Residential Air Purifiers are independent units that can be placed in any room to filter and clean the air. These are ideal for smaller spaces or specific areas that need air purification. On the other hand, In-Duct Residential Air Purifiers are installed within the HVAC system to purify the air throughout the entire home. They are more integrated and provide whole-house air purification. Both types use HEPA filters to effectively remove allergens and pollutants from the air.</p></p>
