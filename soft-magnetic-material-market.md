<p><p>Soft magnetic materials are materials that exhibit magnetic properties only when subjected to a magnetic field and quickly lose their magnetization when the field is removed. These materials are widely used in electrical and electronic devices, including transformers, inductors, motors, and generators. Soft magnetic materials help to efficiently transfer and control magnetic flux in these devices, making them crucial components in the construction of various electrical equipment.</p><p>The global soft magnetic material market is expected to experience steady growth during the forecast period. The increasing demand for energy-efficient devices and the growth of the electronics and automotive industries are driving the market.
