<p><ul><li>Steam Type</li><li>Ram Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rotary Sprinkler System market can be categorized into two main types, namely Steam Type and Ram Type. The Steam Type rotary sprinkler system operates by using steam as a power source to rotate the sprinkler head and distribute water. On the other hand, the Ram Type system utilizes water pressure to move the sprinkler head in a rotary motion. Both types are commonly used in agriculture and landscaping for efficient irrigation of large areas. Buyers can choose between these options based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
