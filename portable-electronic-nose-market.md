<p><strong>The Portable Electronic Nose Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metal Oxide Semiconductor (MOS)</li><li>Conducting polymers(CP)</li><li>Quartz crystal microbalance(QCM)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable electronic nose market consists of various types of technologies such as Metal Oxide Semiconductor (MOS), Conducting polymers (CP), Quartz crystal microbalance (QCM), and other emerging technologies. MOS sensors are based on the conductivity changes of metal oxides in the presence of odorous gases. CP sensors detect odors through changes in electrical conductivity. QCM sensors work on the principle of mass changes due to gas interactions on a quartz crystal surface. Other emerging technologies in the market aim to improve sensitivity and selectivity in odor detection for various applications.</p></p>
