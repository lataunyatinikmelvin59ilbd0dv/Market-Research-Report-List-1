<p><p>Surface protection films are used to protect various surfaces from damage during transportation, handling, and installation. There are two main types of surface protection films: adhesive free films and adhesive films. Adhesive free films are easy to apply and remove without leaving any residue, making them ideal for temporary protection. On the other hand, adhesive films have a sticky backing that adheres to the surface, providing a more secure hold. Both types of films offer protection against scratches, abrasions, and other forms of damage.</p></p>
