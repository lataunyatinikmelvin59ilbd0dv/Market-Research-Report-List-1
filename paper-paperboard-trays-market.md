<p><p>Paper & paperboard trays can be classified into two main types based on the type of fiber used: recycled fiber trays and virgin fiber trays. Recycled fiber trays are made from post-consumer waste paper, offering an environmentally friendly packaging solution. On the other hand, virgin fiber trays are produced from freshly harvested wood pulp, providing a higher level of strength and quality. Both types cater to different market segments, with environmentally conscious consumers opting for recycled fiber trays and industries requiring premium packaging opting for virgin fiber trays.</p></p>
