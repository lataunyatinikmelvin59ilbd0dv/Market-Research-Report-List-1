<p><p>HIV self-test kits are available in three main market types: Lateral Flow Immunoassay, Immunofiltration, and Fluorescent Chromatography. Lateral Flow Immunoassay kits are simple and user-friendly, providing quick results within minutes. Immunofiltration kits use a filter to separate antibodies and antigens for accurate detection. Fluorescent Chromatography kits utilize fluorescent labels for enhanced sensitivity and specificity. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of ease of use, accuracy, and speed of results, catering to different preferences and needs of consumers.</p></p>
