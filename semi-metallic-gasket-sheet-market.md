<p><p>Semi-Metallic Gasket Sheet market has two main types based on their application on flanges: Flat Flange and Non-planar Flange. Flat Flange gaskets are used on smooth, flat surfaces to create a seal, while Non-planar Flange gaskets are designed for use on irregular or non-flat surfaces that require a more flexible sealing solution. Both types of gaskets play a crucial role in preventing leaks and ensuring the integrity of the connection between two mating surfaces in various industrial applications.</p></p>
