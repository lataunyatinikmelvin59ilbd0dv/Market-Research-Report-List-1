Thin films under 0.5 mm are suitable for applications requiring flexibility and transparency. Films in the 0.5-1.0 mm range provide a balance between flexibility and strength. Films above 1.0 mm are thicker and offer greater durability and impact resistance. Each market type caters to different requirements and applications in the photovoltaic industry.</p></p>
