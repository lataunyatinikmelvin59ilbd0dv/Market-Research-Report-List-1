<p><p>Research Spectrometer Market is divided into two main types: Atomic Spectrometer and Molecular Spectrometer. </p><p>Atomic Spectrometers analyze the elemental composition of samples by measuring the absorption or emission of light, which helps in identifying and quantifying elements present in a sample. </p><p>On the other hand, Molecular Spectrometers analyze the molecular structure of compounds by measuring the interaction of molecules with electromagnetic radiation, providing information on chemical bonds, functional groups, and overall molecular structure. Both types of spectrometers play a crucial role in scientific research and analysis.</p></p>
