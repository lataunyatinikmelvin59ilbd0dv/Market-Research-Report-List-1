<p><strong>The Nickel Paste Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Temperature Sintered Nickel Paste</li><li>Medium Temperature Sintered Nickel Paste</li><li>High Temperature Sintered Nickel Paste</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nickel paste is categorized based on the sintering temperature required for the paste to form a solid structure. Low temperature sintered nickel paste has a sintering temperature below 250°C and is used for applications where heat-sensitive components are involved. Medium temperature sintered nickel paste requires a sintering temperature between 250-350°C and is commonly used in electronics and automotive industries. High temperature sintered nickel paste needs sintering temperature above 350°C and is suitable for applications where high thermal and electrical conductivity is required, such as in aerospace and defense sectors.</p></p>
