<p><p>Crop growth regulators market can be segmented into two types based on formulations: wettable powders and solutions. Wettable powders are solid formulations that dissolve in water, creating a suspension that can be easily sprayed onto crops. Solutions, on the other hand, are liquid formulations that are already dissolved and ready for application. Both types of formulations are commonly used in agriculture to promote plant growth, regulate flowering, and enhance fruit set, among other benefits. Each formulation type offers unique benefits for growers depending on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
