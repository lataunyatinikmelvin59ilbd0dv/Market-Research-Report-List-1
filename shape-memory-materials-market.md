<p><p>Shape memory materials are classified into three main types in the market - alloy materials, polymer materials, and others. Alloy materials, such as nickel-titanium alloys, exhibit unique shape memory properties due to their crystalline structure. Polymer materials, on the other hand, can also display shape memory properties through reversible transitions in molecular structure. Other shape memory materials include ceramics, composites, and hybrid materials. Each type of material offers distinct advantages and is utilized in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, healthcare, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
