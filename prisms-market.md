<p><ul><li>Dispersion Prisms</li><li>Deviation Prisms</li><li>Rotation Prisms</li><li>Displacement Prisms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Prisms are optical devices that can be categorized into various market types based on their specific functions. Dispersion prisms are used to separate white light into its component colors. Deviation prisms change the direction of light beams. Rotation prisms rotate the orientation of an image. Displacement prisms shift the position of an image. Each of these prisms serves a unique purpose in manipulating light for different applications, such as spectroscopy, lens systems, and imaging technology.</p></p>
