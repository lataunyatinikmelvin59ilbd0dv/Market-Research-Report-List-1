<p><strong>The Paint Marker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid Type</li><li>Solid Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Paint markers are available in two main types: liquid and solid. Liquid paint markers are filled with a free-flowing paint that allows for smooth application and vibrant colors. They are commonly used for creating detailed artwork on various surfaces. On the other hand, solid paint markers contain dry paint that is activated by shaking to mix with a liquid component. These markers are known for their durability and are often used for outdoor or industrial applications where a permanent and weather-resistant mark is needed.</p></p>
