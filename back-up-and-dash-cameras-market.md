<p><p>Back-up and dash cameras are popular automotive safety devices that come in two main types: single lens and dual lens. Single lens cameras capture footage from one perspective, typically facing forward or backward. Dual lens cameras have two separate lenses, allowing them to simultaneously record both the front and rear of the vehicle. Single lens cameras are more common and affordable, while dual lens cameras provide more comprehensive coverage and are typically used in commercial vehicles or by drivers who want more advanced features.</p></p>
