<p><p>Non-ionic iodine contrast media are substances used in medical imaging procedures to enhance the visibility of certain body structures or fluids. Non-ionic contrast media offer several advantages, such as lower toxicity, reduced risk of adverse reactions, and better compatibility with patients.
