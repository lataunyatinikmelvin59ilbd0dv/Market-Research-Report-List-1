<p><p>Miniature Thermoelectric Generators (TEG) are used to convert heat energy into electricity. There are two main market types for TEGs: Single Stage Module and Multistage Module. </p><p>Single Stage Module TEGs have only one layer of thermoelectric material, making them simpler and more cost-effective but with lower efficiency. </p><p>On the other hand, Multistage Module TEGs consist of multiple layers of thermoelectric material, allowing for higher efficiency and power generation but at a higher cost. Both types of TEGs are used in various applications where a small, efficient power source is needed.</p></p>
