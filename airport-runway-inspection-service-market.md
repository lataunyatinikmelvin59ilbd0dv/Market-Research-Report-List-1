<p><p>Airport runway inspection services can be categorized into two main types: high altitude inspection and ground inspection. High altitude inspection involves using specialized equipment to conduct aerial surveys of the runways and surrounding areas, providing a comprehensive view of potential hazards or issues. Ground inspection, on the other hand, involves physical inspections conducted by trained personnel on foot or using vehicles to identify any defects or debris on the runway surface that could pose a safety risk to aircraft during takeoff and landing.</p></p>
