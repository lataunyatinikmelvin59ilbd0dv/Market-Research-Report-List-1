<p><strong>The Electronic Torquemeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dynamic</li><li>Static</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic torque meters can be classified into two main types - dynamic and static. Dynamic torquemeters are designed to measure torque in rotating machinery, such as engines and turbines, while they are in motion. These devices provide real-time data on the torque being applied. On the other hand, static torquemeters are used to measure torque in stationary equipment, such as pumps and compressors. They are typically used for calibration purposes and can provide accurate torque measurements at a specific moment in time.</p></p>
