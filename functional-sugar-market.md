<p><p>Functional sugar market types include Oligosaccharides, which are made up of 3-10 sugar units and are known for their prebiotic properties. Inulin is another type, known for its ability to promote gut health and improve digestion. Sugar alcohols, such as xylitol and erythritol, are commonly used as low-calorie sweeteners in various food products. Other types like polydextrose and resistant starch also fall under the category of functional sugars, offering various health benefits including improved blood sugar control and increased satiety.</p></p>
