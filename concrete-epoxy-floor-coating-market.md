<p><strong>The Concrete Epoxy Floor Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Epoxy Floor Coating</li><li>Non-Solvent Epoxy Floor Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Concrete epoxy floor coating market can be categorized into solvent-based and non-solvent-based epoxy coatings. Solvent epoxy floor coatings contain volatile organic compounds (VOCs) which evaporate during the curing process. Non-solvent epoxy floor coatings, on the other hand, are environmentally friendly as they do not emit harmful VOCs. Both types of epoxy coatings provide a durable and protective finish for concrete floors, but non-solvent epoxy coatings are preferred for their eco-friendly properties.</p></p>
