<p><p>Smart textiles for wearable technology can be categorized into three main types: passive smart, active smart, and ultra-smart. Passive smart textiles are capable of sensing and responding to environmental stimuli, such as temperature or moisture. Active smart textiles can actively change their properties in response to stimuli, such as adjusting insulation or altering color. Ultra-smart textiles incorporate advanced technologies, such as integrated sensors, actuators, and communication capabilities, allowing for high levels of interactivity and functionality. These three types cater to different levels of complexity and functionality in the smart textiles market.</p></p>
