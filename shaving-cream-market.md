<p><p>Shaving cream market can be categorized into aftershave and pre-shave products. Aftershave products are used after shaving to soothe and hydrate the skin, preventing irritation and razor burn. They often contain ingredients like aloe vera and vitamin E. On the other hand, pre-shave products are used before shaving to prepare the skin and hair for a smoother shave. They typically include oils and lubricants to soften the hair and provide a protective barrier against the razor. Both aftershave and pre-shave products play important roles in a complete shaving routine.</p></p>
