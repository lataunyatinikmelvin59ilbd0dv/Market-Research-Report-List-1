<p><p>Manufactured sand market includes two main types: Mixed Sand and Crushed Sand. Mixed Sand is a blend of natural sand and crushed stone particles, often used in concrete production due to its superior workability and strength. Crushed Sand is produced by crushing hard stones such as granite or basalt into fine particles, commonly used as a substitute for natural sand in construction projects. Both types of manufactured sand offer advantages like consistent quality, better durability, and reduced environmental impact compared to traditional river sand.</p></p>
