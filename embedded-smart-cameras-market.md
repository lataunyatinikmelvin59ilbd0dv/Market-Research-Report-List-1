<p><p>Embedded smart cameras are compact devices equipped with imaging sensors, processors, and communication interfaces that allow them to capture, process, and transmit images and videos in real-time. This growth is attributed to the increasing demand for intelligent surveillance systems, rising adoption of Industry 4.0 technologies in the manufacturing sector, and advancements in artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms for image processing and analysis.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the embedded smart cameras market is the integration of edge computing capabilities, which allows these devices to process and analyze data locally without needing to transmit it to a central server. This enables faster response times and reduces bandwidth requirements. Another trend is the increasing use of deep learning algorithms for object detection, recognition, and tracking, which improves the accuracy and reliability of video analytics applications.</p></p>
