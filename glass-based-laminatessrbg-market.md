<p><p>Glass Based Laminates (SRBG) are composite materials made of glass fabric impregnated with resin, providing high strength and durability. There are four main types of SRBGs based on the resin used: Phenolic, Epoxy, Silicone, and Polyimide. Phenolic laminates offer good electrical insulation, epoxy laminates have high mechanical strength, silicone laminates provide thermal resistance, and polyimide laminates have superior heat resistance. Each type of SRBG has its own unique properties and applications in various industries such as electronics, aerospace, and automotive.</p></p>
