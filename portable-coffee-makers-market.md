<p><p>Portable coffee makers come in two main market types: manual and automatic. Manual coffee makers require the user to physically control the brewing process, such as hand-pumping or pressing down on a plunger. These types are often favored by coffee enthusiasts for the control they offer over the brewing process. Automatic coffee makers, on the other hand, do the brewing process with minimal user input, often using pre-programmed settings for consistency and convenience. Both types cater to different preferences in terms of control and ease of use.</p></p>
