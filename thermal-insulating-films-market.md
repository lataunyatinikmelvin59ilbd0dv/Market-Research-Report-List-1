<p><ul><li>Magnetron Sputtering Technology</li><li>Metal Evaporation Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal insulating films market includes two main types of technologies: Magnetron Sputtering and Metal Evaporation. Magnetron Sputtering Technology involved the deposition of a metal or compound film onto a substrate using high-power plasma, creating a thin, uniform film with excellent properties for thermal insulation. Metal Evaporation Technology, on the other hand, involves heating a metal until it evaporates and then condenses onto a substrate, forming a thin film with good insulating properties. Both technologies play a crucial role in the development of advanced thermal insulation solutions.</p></p>
