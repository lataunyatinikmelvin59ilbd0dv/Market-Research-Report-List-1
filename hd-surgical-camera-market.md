<p><p>HD Surgical Camera Market encompasses the two main types of camera technologies: CMOS and CCD. CMOS cameras are known for their high resolution and fast processing speed, making them ideal for surgical procedures that require real-time imaging. On the other hand, CCD cameras are known for their excellent image quality and sensitivity in low-light conditions, making them suitable for more complex surgeries. Both technologies are widely used in the healthcare industry for a variety of surgical applications due to their unique advantages and capabilities.</p></p>
