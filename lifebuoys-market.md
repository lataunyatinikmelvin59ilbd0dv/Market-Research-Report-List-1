<p><p>Inherent life Lifebuoys are designed to provide buoyancy without the need for inflation, making them reliable in emergency situations. They are made of durable material that allows them to float and support the weight of a person in water. On the other hand, Inflatable life Lifebuoys require manual inflation using a CO2 cartridge or by blowing into a tube. They are lightweight and compact when deflated, making them easy to store and transport. Both types of lifebuoys are essential safety devices for water activities and emergencies.</p></p>
