Two main types of coatings dominate the market, powder coatings and liquid coatings. Powder coatings are dry, free-flowing powders that are applied electrostatically and then cured with heat. Liquid coatings, on the other hand, are liquid paints that are typically applied with a brush, roller, or spray gun. Each type of coating offers its own advantages and should be chosen based on the specific requirements of the project.</p></p>
