<p><p>Intravascular Ultrasound (IVUS) Devices are imaging devices used in cardiology to visualize the inside of blood vessels in order to diagnose and treat various cardiovascular diseases. IVUS devices use a small ultrasound probe inserted into the blood vessel to generate real-time images, allowing healthcare professionals to assess the vessel's structure, plaque build-up, and blood flow.</p><p>The Intravascular Ultrasound (IVUS) Devices Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8% during the forecast period. The market growth is driven by the increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, rising adoption of minimally invasive procedures, and technological advancements in IVUS devices. Moreover, the growing geriatric population and the rising demand for early diagnosis and treatment of cardiovascular diseases are also contributing to the market growth.</p><p>The latest trends in the IVUS devices market include the development of advanced imaging technologies, such as virtual histology IVUS and optical coherence tomography (OCT) IVUS, which provide more detailed and accurate imaging of blood vessels. Additionally, the increasing focus on personalized medicine and the integration of artificial intelligence in IVUS devices are expected to drive market growth in the coming years.</p></p>
