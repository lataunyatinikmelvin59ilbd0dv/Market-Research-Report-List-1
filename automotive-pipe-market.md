Welded pipes are manufactured by welding together sections of steel while seamless pipes are made from a single piece of steel without any welding. Welded pipes are more commonly used in automotive applications due to their lower cost, while seamless pipes are preferred for applications where a higher level of durability and strength is required.</p></p>
