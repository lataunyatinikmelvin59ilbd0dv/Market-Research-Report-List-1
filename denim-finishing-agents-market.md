<p><p>Denim finishing agents refer to various chemical substances used to enhance the appearance, durability, and feel of denim fabrics. Enzymes are commonly used to achieve fading effects and create distressed looks. Resins are applied to improve crease resistance and stiffness. Softeners are used to provide a softer feel. Defoamers minimize foam during the manufacturing process. Bleaching agents are utilized to achieve a desired color or fade effect. Crush-resistant agents help maintain the fabric's shape. Anti-back staining agents prevent dye transfer during laundry. Other agents include flame retardants, water repellents, and abrasion-resistant additives.</p></p>
