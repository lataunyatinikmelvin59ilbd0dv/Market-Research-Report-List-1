<p><p>Advanced Wafer Probe Cards are essential tools for testing semiconductor devices during the manufacturing process. These cards come in various types including Needle Type, Vertical Type, and MEMS Type. Needle Type probe cards use sharp needles to make contact with the wafer, offering high accuracy and precision. Vertical Type probe cards provide vertical contact with the wafer surface, enabling efficient testing of small features. MEMS Type probe cards utilize Microelectromechanical Systems technology for enhanced testing capabilities, making them well-suited for advanced semiconductor applications.</p></p>
