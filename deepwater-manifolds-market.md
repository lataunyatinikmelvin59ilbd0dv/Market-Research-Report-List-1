<p><ul><li>Template Manifold</li><li>Cluster Manifold</li><li>Pipeline End Manifold</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Deepwater manifolds are crucial components in subsea oil and gas production systems. Template manifolds are standardized structures that can support multiple wells, while cluster manifolds are used to connect multiple flowlines from subsea wells to a central gathering point. Pipeline end manifolds are located at the end of production pipelines to distribute the flow of hydrocarbons. Other types of manifolds may include hot-tap manifolds, injection manifolds, and pig launchers/receivers, each serving specific functions in deepwater production systems.</p></p>
