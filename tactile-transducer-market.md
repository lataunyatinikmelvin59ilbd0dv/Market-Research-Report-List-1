<p><strong>The Tactile Transducer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Eccentric Rotating Mass (ERM) Actuators</li><li>Linear Resonant Actuators (LRAS)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tactile transducer market types include Eccentric Rotating Mass (ERM) Actuators, Linear Resonant Actuators (LRAs), and Others. ERM actuators use an unbalanced mass rotating on an eccentric shaft to create vibrations. LRAs use a voice coil motor with a precision linear resonant system to produce vibrations. Other types of tactile transducers may utilize different mechanisms or technologies to generate tactile feedback, offering a variety of options for manufacturers and consumers looking for haptic solutions in their devices.</p></p>
