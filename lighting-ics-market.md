<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Lighting ICs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AC-DC LED Driver IC</li><li>DC-DC LED Driver IC</li><li>LED Dimming Driver IC</li><li>Linear LED Driver IC</li><li>Ballast IC</li><li>NFC IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lighting ICs market is divided into various types based on the functionality they provide. AC-DC LED Driver ICs are used to power LEDs from AC source. DC-DC LED Driver ICs are used for DC power supplies. LED Dimming Driver ICs enable dimming capabilities in LEDs. Linear LED Driver ICs provide constant current output for LEDs. Ballast ICs are used for fluorescent and HID lighting. NFC ICs enable communication and control of lighting systems through NFC technology. Each type of IC serves a specific purpose in the lighting industry.</p></p>
