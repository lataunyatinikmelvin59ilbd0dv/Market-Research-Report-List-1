<p><p>Inorganic binders play a key role in various industrial applications, with different types including silicate, phosphate, oxides, sulfate, and borates. Silicate binders are known for their high strength and resistance to temperature and chemicals. Phosphate binders offer excellent adhesion and corrosion resistance. Oxide binders provide high stability and durability. Sulfate binders are valued for their fire resistance and water repellent properties. Borate binders are widely used for their low toxicity and excellent thermal insulating properties. Each type offers unique characteristics to cater to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
