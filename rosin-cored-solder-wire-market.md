<p><strong>The Rosin Cored Solder Wire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inactive Rosin</li><li>Rosin Activated (RA)</li><li>Rosin Mildly Activated (RMA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rosin cored solder wire is available in different types such as Inactive Rosin, Rosin Activated (RA), and Rosin Mildly Activated (RMA) based on the level of flux activity. Inactive Rosin contains no activators and is suitable for non-sensitive electronic components. RA has a higher level of activators, providing strong cleaning and fluxing properties. RMA falls in between the two, offering moderate fluxing capabilities. Users can choose the appropriate type based on the soldering requirements and component sensitivity.</p></p>
