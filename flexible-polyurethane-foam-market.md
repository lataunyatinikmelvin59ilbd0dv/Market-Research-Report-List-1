<p><p>Flexible Polyurethane Foam comes in various types to cater to different needs and applications. High Rebound Foam(HRF) offers exceptional bounce back properties. Tired Polyurethane Foam is used for cushioning and comfort in mattresses and furniture. Integral Skin Foam(ISF) is known for its smooth and durable outer layer. Massive Sponge is a soft and absorbent foam used for cleaning and packaging. Semi-Rigid Energy-Absorbing Foam provides impact resistance and protection in automotive and sports equipment industries. Each type serves a specific purpose in the market for flexible polyurethane foam.</p></p>
