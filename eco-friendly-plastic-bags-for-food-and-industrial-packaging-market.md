<p><p>Eco-friendly plastic bags for food and industrial packaging can be categorized into several types. Polylactic acid (PLA) bags are derived from renewable resources like corn starch and are compostable. Polyhydroxyalkanoate (PHA) bags are biodegradable and produced from microbial fermentation. Starch blends are a combination of different starch sources and biodegradable polymers. Other types of eco-friendly plastic bags may include bags made from recycled materials or biodegradable additives. Each type offers a sustainable alternative to traditional plastic bags, reducing environmental impact.</p></p>
