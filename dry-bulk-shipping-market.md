<p><p>In the dry bulk shipping market, there are two main market types: time charter and voyage market. Time charter refers to the agreement between a shipowner and a charterer where the ship is chartered for a specific period at an agreed-upon daily rate. This allows the charterer to have full control over the ship's itinerary and cargo. On the other hand, the voyage market involves a one-time agreement where the ship is contracted to transport a specific cargo from one port to another for an agreed price. The charterer has less control over the ship's itinerary in the voyage market.</p></p>
