<p><p>The II-VI Compound Semiconductor Market includes various types based on deposition technology such as CVD, MBE, HVPE, MOVPE, and LPE. Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) involves the deposition of thin films using chemical reactions. Molecular Beam Epitaxy (MBE) is a technique for growing crystalline layers. Hydride Vapor Phase Epitaxy (HVPE) uses chemical reactions in a gas phase. Metalorganic Vapor Phase Epitaxy (MOVPE) involves the deposition of materials from a gas phase. Liquid Phase Epitaxy (LPE) is a method for growing crystal layers from a liquid phase.</p></p>
