<p><p>Digital drums come in three main market types based on the type of heads used on the drum pads - Rubber heads, Mylar heads, and Mesh or Silicone heads. Rubber heads are known for their durability and resistance to wear and tear, while Mylar heads provide a more authentic acoustic drum feel. Mesh or Silicone heads offer a more realistic response and sensitive touch, mimicking the feel of playing on an acoustic drum kit. Each type caters to different preferences and playing styles in the digital drums market.</p></p>
