<p><p>Reverse Osmosis (RO) Membrane market includes two primary types: Composite Membranes and Asymmetric Membranes. Composite Membranes are made up of multiple layers, typically consisting of a thin film composite that provides high rejection rates and durability. Asymmetric Membranes have a dense selective layer on one side and a porous support layer on the other, allowing for better permeability and flux rates. Both types play a crucial role in water treatment processes, providing efficient filtration and purification for various applications.</p></p>
