<p><ul><li>Direct Printing</li><li>Disperse Printing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital printed fashion fabrics market is categorized into two main types: direct printing and disperse printing. Direct printing involves printing the design directly onto the fabric, resulting in vibrant colors and intricate designs. Disperse printing, on the other hand, involves transferring the design from paper onto the fabric using heat and pressure, which is ideal for synthetic fabrics. Both types of printing offer unique benefits and are preferred based on the fabric composition and design requirements of the fashion industry.</p></p>
