<p><strong>The Embedded Optical Fiber Polarizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SM Type</li><li>PM Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Embedded Optical Fiber Polarizer market can be divided into two main types - SM Type and PM Type. SM Type polarizers are single-mode fibers that are designed to only allow light polarized in a specific direction to pass through. PM Type polarizers, on the other hand, are polarization maintaining fibers that are able to maintain the polarization state of light traveling through them. Both types of polarizers are important components in optical communication systems and have various applications in industries such as telecommunications and data transmission.</p></p>
