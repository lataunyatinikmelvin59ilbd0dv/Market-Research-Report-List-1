<p><p>Alkyd coatings can be classified into three main types based on their drying properties: non-drying coatings, drying coatings, and semi-drying coatings. Non-drying coatings do not harden or cure once applied, making them ideal for applications where a flexible finish is desired. Drying coatings dry through oxidation and form a hard finish, while semi-drying coatings exhibit properties of both non-drying and drying coatings. Each type of alkyd coating offers unique benefits and is used in various industries based on their specific characteristics.</p></p>
