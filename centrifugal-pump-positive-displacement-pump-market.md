<p><p>Centrifugal pumps are a type of pump that uses rotating impellers to increase fluid velocity and convert kinetic energy into potential energy. They come in various types such as axial flow pumps, which move fluids parallel to the pump shaft; mixed or radial flow pumps, which combine axial and radial flow; peripheral pumps, which use rotating vanes; and jet pumps that create suction by expelling fluid at high pressure. Positive displacement pumps, on the other hand, move fluid by trapping and then displacing a fixed amount of volume, offering different types and designs for various applications.</p></p>
