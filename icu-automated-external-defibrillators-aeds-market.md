<p><p>ICU Automated External Defibrillators (AEDs) come in two main types - semi-automated and fully automated. Semi-automated AEDs require the user to press a button to deliver a shock to the patient, while fully automated AEDs deliver the shock automatically without the need for user intervention. Both types are designed to quickly analyze a patient's heart rhythm and deliver a potentially life-saving shock to restore normal rhythm in cases of sudden cardiac arrest. These devices are crucial in critical care settings such as ICUs.</p></p>
