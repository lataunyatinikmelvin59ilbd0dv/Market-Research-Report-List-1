<p><p>Specialty chocolate market includes various types such as white chocolate and dark chocolate. White chocolate is made from cocoa butter, sugar, and milk solids, creating a creamy and sweet flavor. It does not contain cocoa solids. Dark chocolate, on the other hand, is made from cocoa liquor, cocoa butter, and sugar, with a higher percentage of cocoa solids. It has a rich and intense flavor with varying levels of bitterness depending on the cocoa content. Both types cater to different consumer preferences in the luxury chocolate market.</p></p>
