<p><p>Reflex type photoelectric sensors are used in various industries for sensing objects and materials. The market includes different types such as retroreflective photoelectric sensors and diffuse reflective photoelectric sensors. Retroreflective sensors use a reflector to bounce light back to the sensor, making them ideal for longer distance sensing applications. Diffuse reflective sensors detect objects by sensing the light reflected off the object itself, eliminating the need for a separate reflector. Both types of sensors provide reliable and accurate detection capabilities for a wide range of industrial applications.</p></p>
