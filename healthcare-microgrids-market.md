<p><p>Healthcare microgrids are categorized into three main types: AC microgrid system, DC microgrid system, and hybrid microgrid system. AC microgrid systems utilize alternating current for power distribution, while DC microgrid systems use direct current. Hybrid microgrid systems combine both AC and DC technologies for optimal power distribution. Each type of system offers unique benefits and considerations for healthcare facilities looking to improve energy efficiency and reliability. These diverse options allow healthcare organizations to choose the best microgrid solution based on their specific needs and resources.</p></p>
