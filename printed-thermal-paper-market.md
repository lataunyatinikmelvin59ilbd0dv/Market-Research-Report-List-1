<p><p>Printed thermal paper is a type of paper that is coated with a chemical that changes color when exposed to heat. It is commonly used in point-of-sale systems, credit card terminals, and fax machines. The print on thermal paper is created by applying heat to the paper, eliminating the need for ink or toner.</p><p>The printed thermal paper market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.40% during the forecast period. The growth of the market is attributed to the increasing adoption of thermal paper in various industries such as retail, healthcare, transportation, and entertainment. The convenience of thermal paper, along with its affordability and eco-friendliness, are driving its demand in the market.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the printed thermal paper market is the increasing demand for eco-friendly thermal paper. Manufacturers are developing thermal paper that is free of harmful chemicals such as BPA (bisphenol A) to meet the growing consumer demand for environmentally friendly products.
