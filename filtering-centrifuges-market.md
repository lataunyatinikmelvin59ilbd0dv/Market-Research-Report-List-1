<p><ul><li>Horizontal Centrifuges</li><li>Vertical Centrifuges</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Filtering centrifuges are classified into two main types based on their orientation: horizontal centrifuges and vertical centrifuges.</p><p>Horizontal centrifuges have a horizontal axis of rotation and are commonly used for separating solid particles from liquids in industries such as wastewater treatment and chemical processing. They are efficient for continuous operation and offer high throughput.</p><p>Vertical centrifuges, on the other hand, have a vertical axis of rotation and are typically used for separating liquids from solids. They are well-suited for applications where gentle handling of delicate materials is required.</p></p>
