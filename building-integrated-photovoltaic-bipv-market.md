<p><p>Building Integrated Photovoltaic (BIPV) utilizes solar panels that are integrated directly into the building's design and structure, providing both energy production and aesthetic appeal. There are two main types of BIPV technologies: Crystalline Silicon and Thin Film. Crystalline Silicon panels are made of silicon wafers, offering high efficiency and durability. Thin Film panels are made by depositing a thin layer of photovoltaic material onto a substrate, making them lightweight and flexible. Both types are gaining popularity in the market for their ability to seamlessly blend in with building architecture while generating renewable energy.</p></p>
