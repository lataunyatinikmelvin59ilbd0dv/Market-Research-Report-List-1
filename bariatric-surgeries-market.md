<p><p>Bariatric surgeries are medical procedures that help people with severe obesity to lose weight by reducing the size of their stomach. There are several types of bariatric surgeries available in the market. Gastric bypass surgery involves creating a smaller stomach pouch and rerouting the digestive tract. Sleeve gastrectomy surgery removes a large portion of the stomach to create a smaller and banana-shaped stomach. Gastric banding surgery involves placing an adjustable band around the upper part of the stomach to decrease its capacity. Other types of bariatric surgeries include gastric plication and biliopancreatic diversion.</p></p>
