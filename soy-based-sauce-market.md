<p><strong>The Soy based Sauce Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brewed Soy-based Sauce</li><li>Blended Soy-based Sauce</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Brewed Soy-based Sauce is a traditional type of soy sauce that is made by fermenting soybeans, wheat, salt, and water over a period of several months to develop a complex flavor profile. On the other hand, Blended Soy-based Sauce is a newer type of soy sauce that is made by combining different ingredients such as hydrolyzed vegetable protein, corn syrup, and caramel color to achieve a more consistent and less time-consuming production process. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and culinary uses in the market.</p></p>
