<p><p>Die bonding paste market consists of various types including no-clean pastes, rosin-based pastes, water-soluble pastes, and other specialized pastes. No-clean pastes leave minimal residue after bonding, making them ideal for sensitive applications. Rosin-based pastes offer strong adhesion and compatibility with a wide range of materials. Water-soluble pastes are easily removed with water, making them environmentally friendly. Other specialized pastes cater to specific industry requirements, such as high-temperature resistance or faster curing times. Each type of paste serves a unique purpose in the die bonding process.</p></p>
