<p><strong>The Medical Audiological Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Behind-the-ear hearing aids (BTE)</li><li>Receiver in the canal hearing aids (RITE)</li><li>In the canal hearing aids (IIC)</li><li>Completely in the canal hearing aids (CIC)</li><li>Body-worn hearing aids</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical Audiological Devices Market Types include various options to cater to different hearing needs. Behind-the-ear hearing aids (BTE), are worn behind the ear. Receiver in the canal hearing aids (RITE) have a receiver in the ear canal. In the canal hearing aids (IIC) are smaller and fit partially in the ear canal. Completely in the canal hearing aids (CIC) fit entirely inside the ear canal. Body-worn hearing aids are worn on the body, while others include options like invisible hearing aids and disposable hearing aids.</p></p>
