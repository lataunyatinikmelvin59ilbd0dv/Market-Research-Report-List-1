<p><p>Electronic Caries Monitors (ECM) are devices used for detecting and monitoring dental caries (tooth decay) in its early stages. These devices use technology such as electrical resistance measurement, laser fluorescence, and impedance spectroscopy to detect changes in tooth enamel and identify areas of decay. The use of ECMs can help dentists in diagnosing caries at an earlier stage, enabling them to provide timely treatment and prevent further damage to the teeth.</p><p>The Electronic Caries Monitors (ECM) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.4% during the forecast period.
