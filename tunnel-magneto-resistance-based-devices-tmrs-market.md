<p><p>Tunnel Magneto Resistance Based Devices (TMRs) utilize two key market types: Clockwise Spin and Counter Clockwise Spin. In Clockwise Spin, the electrons spin in a clockwise direction, resulting in a lower resistance state. On the other hand, Counter Clockwise Spin involves electrons spinning in the opposite direction, leading to a higher resistance state. These market types are essential for the performance of TMRs as they determine the level of resistance and ultimately impact the efficiency and functionality of the devices.</p></p>
