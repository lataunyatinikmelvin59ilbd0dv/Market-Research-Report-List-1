<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Composite Phase Wire (OPPC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Central Tube Structure OPPC</li><li>Layer Stranding Structure OPPC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Fiber Composite Phase Wire (OPPC) is a type of overhead power line that combines optical fibers for communication with electrical conductors for power transmission. There are two main types of OPPC: Central Tube Structure OPPC, where optical fibers are placed in a central tube surrounded by conductors, and Layer Stranding Structure OPPC, where optical fibers are embedded within the layers of conductors. Both types offer improved communication capabilities and reliability compared to traditional power lines.</p></p>
