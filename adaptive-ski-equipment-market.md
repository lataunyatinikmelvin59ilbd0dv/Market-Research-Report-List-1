<p><p>Adaptive ski equipment includes monoskis and dualskis, which are specifically designed for individuals with physical disabilities to enjoy skiing. Monoskis have a single wide ski in the center with a chair attached, allowing for more maneuverability and control. Dualskis consist of two skis with a seat in the middle, allowing an individual with a disability to ski with the assistance of a guide or instructor. Both types of equipment cater to the unique needs and abilities of individuals with disabilities, providing them with a safe and enjoyable skiing experience.</p></p>
