<p><strong>The Automotive Fly Wheel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single mass flywheel</li><li>Dual mass flywheel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive flywheel market consists of two main types - single mass flywheel and dual mass flywheel. Single mass flywheels are simpler in design, consisting of a single piece of metal that directly connects the engine to the transmission. Dual mass flywheels are more complex, with two separate flywheels connected by a torsional spring system that absorbs engine vibrations. Single mass flywheels are typically more durable and offer better performance, while dual mass flywheels provide smoother operation and reduce engine noise and vibrations.</p></p>
