<p><p>One-sided sealing miniature ball bearings have a dust cover on one side only, providing protection against external contaminants entering the bearing. This type of bearing is suitable for applications where only one side needs to be sealed. On the other hand, double-sided sealing bearings have dust covers on both sides, offering increased protection against dirt, dust, and moisture from entering the bearing. Double-sided sealing bearings are ideal for applications where sealing on both sides is necessary for optimal performance and longevity.</p></p>
