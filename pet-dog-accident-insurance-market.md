<p><p>Pet dog accident insurance comes in two main market types: lifetime cover and non-lifetime cover. Lifetime cover provides continuous coverage for an illness or injury throughout the dog's life, with a set annual limit for claims. Non-lifetime cover, on the other hand, only offers coverage for a specified period of time or until a maximum payout limit is reached. Lifetime cover is often more expensive but provides more comprehensive protection, while non-lifetime cover may be more affordable but offers limited coverage.</p></p>
