<p><strong>The Soft-Rock Shiled Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Slurry Shield (SS)</li><li>Earth Pressure Balance Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Soft-Rock Shield Machines are commonly used in tunneling projects for softer ground conditions. There are two main types of Soft-Rock Shield Machines: Slurry Shield (SS) and Earth Pressure Balance Machines. Slurry Shield Machines are used for unstable, watery ground conditions where slurry is injected to stabilize the tunnel face. Earth Pressure Balance Machines are used for cohesive soil and rock conditions where the pressure is balanced between the excavation and the surrounding soil to prevent collapses.</p></p>
