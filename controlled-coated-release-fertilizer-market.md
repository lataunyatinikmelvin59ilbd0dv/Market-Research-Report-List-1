<p><p>Controlled (Coated) Release Fertilizer market consists of two main types: Polymer-Coated Products and Non Polymer-Coated Products. Polymer-Coated Products have a protective layer around the fertilizer to control the release rate, making them suitable for long-term use. Non Polymer-Coated Products rely on other mechanisms like chemical reactions or physical barriers to regulate the release of nutrients to plants. Both types cater to different needs in agriculture, with Polymer-Coated Products offering precise control and Non Polymer-Coated Products providing more cost-effective options.</p></p>
