<p><p>The wireless industrial router market can be divided into two main types: wall-mount and embedded routers. Wall-mount routers are designed to be mounted on walls or other surfaces in industrial environments, providing reliable wireless connectivity for monitoring and controlling devices. On the other hand, embedded routers are integrated into other industrial equipment, offering seamless wireless communication capabilities without the need for a separate device. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing connectivity and efficiency in industrial settings.</p></p>
