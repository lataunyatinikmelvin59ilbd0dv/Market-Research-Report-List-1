<p><p>Rotation torque sensors can be classified into dynamic type and static type. Dynamic type sensors are designed to measure torque during rotational motion and are ideal for applications where real-time torque measurements are required. On the other hand, static type sensors are used to measure torque in stationary or low-speed applications. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, where precise torque measurements are essential for quality control and process optimization.</p></p>
