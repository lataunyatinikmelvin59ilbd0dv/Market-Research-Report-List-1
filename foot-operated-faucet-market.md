<p><p>Foot Operated Faucet market consists of two main types - Contact Foot Pedal and Pressed Foot Pedal. Contact Foot Pedal faucets require users to make continuous contact with the pedal to release water flow, making them ideal for public spaces to prevent water wastage. Pressed Foot Pedal faucets only require a single press to activate, providing a more convenient and hygienic option for residential and commercial use. Both types offer hands-free operation for improved hygiene and water conservation.</p></p>
