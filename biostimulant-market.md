<p><p>Biostimulant market types can be categorized into acid-based, extract-based, and others. Acid-based biostimulants contain humic or fulvic acids which help improve soil structure and nutrient uptake. Extract-based biostimulants are derived from natural sources like seaweed or plant extracts, and promote plant growth and stress resistance. Other types of biostimulants may include microbial-based products or organic fertilizers. Each type offers unique benefits for enhancing plant growth, improving crop yield, and increasing overall plant health in various agricultural settings.</p></p>
