<p><strong>The Outdoor LED Displays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Mounted</li><li>Individually Mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Outdoor LED displays can be classified into two main types: surface-mounted and individually mounted. Surface-mounted LED displays are made up of LED modules that are directly mounted onto a flat surface, creating a seamless and sleek appearance. On the other hand, individually mounted LED displays are made up of individual LED pixels mounted onto a grid-like structure, allowing for a more detailed and customizable display. Both types of displays are widely used in outdoor advertising, events, and digital signage applications.</p></p>
