<p><p>The liquid handling market includes three main types: electronic, automated, and manual systems. Electronic liquid handlers utilize technology to dispense precise volumes of liquid quickly and accurately. Automated systems are designed for high-throughput applications, allowing for increased efficiency and reduced human error. Manual systems involve human operators physically transferring liquids using pipettes or other tools. Each type of liquid handling system has its own unique advantages and is used in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and research laboratories.</p></p>
