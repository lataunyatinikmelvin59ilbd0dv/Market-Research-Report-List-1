<p><ul><li>Thermal CTP</li><li>UV CTP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flexo Plate Setters are used in printing industry to make printing plates for flexographic printing. There are two main types of Flexo Plate Setters in the market - Thermal CTP and UV CTP. Thermal CTP systems use heat to create the image on the plate, while UV CTP systems use ultraviolet light. Thermal CTP systems are known for their high resolution and consistent performance, while UV CTP systems are preferred for their fast processing speed and environmentally-friendly nature due to the lack of chemicals involved in the process.</p></p>
