<p><p>Photonic crystals market can be categorized into three types based on their dimensional structure: Three-Dimensional Photonic Crystals, Two-Dimensional Photonic Crystals, and One-Dimensional Photonic Crystals. Three-dimensional photonic crystals are periodic structures that exhibit a photonic band gap in three dimensions, while two-dimensional photonic crystals have a photonic band gap in only two dimensions. One-dimensional photonic crystals are periodic structures that exhibit a photonic band gap in only one dimension. Each type of photonic crystal offers unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
