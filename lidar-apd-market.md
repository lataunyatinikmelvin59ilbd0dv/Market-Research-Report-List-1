<p><strong>The LiDAR APD Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>InGaAs APD</li><li>Silicon APD</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LiDAR APD market types include InGaAs APD, Silicon APD, and Others. InGaAs APDs are preferred for their high sensitivity in the near-infrared spectrum, making them suitable for long-range LiDAR applications. Silicon APDs are widely used due to their lower cost and compatibility with standard CMOS processes, making them ideal for automotive LiDAR systems. The Others category may include alternative materials such as Geiger-mode APDs or custom-designed APDs tailored for specific LiDAR applications. Each type offers unique advantages based on performance, cost, and compatibility requirements.</p></p>
