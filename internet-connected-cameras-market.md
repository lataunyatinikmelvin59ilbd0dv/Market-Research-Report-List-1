<p><p>Internet connected cameras can be divided into two main market types: wired WiFi cameras and wireless WiFi cameras. Wired WiFi cameras require a physical connection to a power source and a WiFi router, providing a stable and reliable connection for consistent video streaming. On the other hand, wireless WiFi cameras operate without the need for cables, allowing for easier installation and greater flexibility in camera placement. Both types offer remote monitoring and surveillance capabilities, with varying levels of convenience and versatility.</p></p>
