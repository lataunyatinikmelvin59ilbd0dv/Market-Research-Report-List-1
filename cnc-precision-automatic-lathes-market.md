<p><p>CNC Precision Automatic Lathes are machines used in manufacturing to shape materials through rotary cutting. Horizontal lathes have the material mounted horizontally and the cutting tool moves in a side-to-side motion. Vertical lathes have the material mounted vertically and the cutting tool moves up and down. Horizontal lathes are ideal for long, thin workpieces, while vertical lathes are better suited for large, heavy pieces. Both types of lathes offer high precision and efficiency in production processes.</p></p>
