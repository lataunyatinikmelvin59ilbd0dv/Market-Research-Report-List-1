<p><p>The Mushroom Fermenter Market can be categorized into two types: Non-mechanical Agitation and Mechanical Type. </p><p>Non-mechanical Agitation refers to a type of mushroom fermenter that does not rely on any mechanical apparatus to mix or agitate the mushroom substrate during the fermentation process. Instead, it uses methods such as manual stirring or natural convection to ensure proper mixing and aeration.</p><p>On the other hand, the Mechanical Type market includes mushroom fermenters that utilize mechanical agitation systems. These fermenters are equipped with mechanical stirrers or mixing devices to actively mix and agitate the mushroom substrate, ensuring uniform distribution of nutrients and oxygen for optimal fermentation.</p></p>
