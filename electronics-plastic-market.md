<p><p>Electronics plastic market can be categorized into two types based on their chemical composition - halogenated and non-halogenated. Halogenated plastics contain elements like fluorine, chlorine, bromine, or iodine, which provide flame retardant properties but can release harmful gases when burned. Non-halogenated plastics do not contain these elements and are considered more environmentally friendly. Both types have their advantages and disadvantages, with halogenated plastics offering better fire resistance but non-halogenated plastics being safer for the environment.</p></p>
