<p><p>Industrial clay market is segmented into different types based on mineral composition. Kaolin, known for its whiteness and fine particle size, is widely used in paper, ceramic, and rubber industries. Smectite clay, with its swellable properties, is used in drilling fluids and as a binder in foundry sands. Illite clay is utilized for its absorbent properties in agriculture and cosmetics. Chlorite clay finds application in drilling fluids and as a sealant in construction. Other types of industrial clays include montmorillonite and vermiculite, each with specific industrial applications.</p></p>
