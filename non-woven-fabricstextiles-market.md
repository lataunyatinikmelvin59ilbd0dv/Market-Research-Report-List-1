<p><p>Non-woven fabrics/textiles market includes various types such as Spunmelt, Wet Laid, Dry Laid, and Others. Spunmelt fabrics are produced by spinning and melting polymers, making them strong and durable. Wet Laid fabrics are created by mixing fibers in water and then laying them out to dry. Dry Laid fabrics are formed by randomly laying fibers and bonding them with heat or chemicals. Other types of non-woven fabrics include needle punch, thermal bonded, and chemical bonded fabrics, each offering unique characteristics and applications.</p></p>
