<p><strong>The Time Temperature Indicators (TTIs) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full History Indicators</li><li>Partial History Indicators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Time Temperature Indicators (TTIs) are used to monitor the temperature exposure of perishable goods during storage and transportation. Full History Indicators provide a continuous record of the temperature exposure from production to consumption, giving complete visibility into the product's history. Partial History Indicators, on the other hand, only show a portion of the temperature exposure, typically from a specific point in time. Both types of TTIs help ensure the quality and safety of perishable products by alerting users to any temperature breaches that could impact their shelf life.</p></p>
