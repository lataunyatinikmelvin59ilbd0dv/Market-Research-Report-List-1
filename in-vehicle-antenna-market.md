<p><p>In-vehicle antennas come in various types such as fin, rod, screen type, film type, integrated, and other types. Fin antennas are usually mounted on the roof of the vehicle for better reception. Rod antennas are long and thin, often seen on older vehicles. Screen type antennas are integrated into the windshield or rear window. Film type antennas are thin and flexible, typically found on side windows. Integrated antennas are built into the vehicle's body. Other types include patch antennas, whip antennas, and shark fin antennas.</p></p>
