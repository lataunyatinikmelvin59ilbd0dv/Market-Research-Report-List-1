<p><p>Reflective packaging films come in different types such as Glass Beads Type Reflective Films and Microprism Type Reflective Films. Glass Beads Type films feature glass beads embedded into the film surface, reflecting light back towards the source. Microprism Type films utilize microprisms to reflect light at various angles. Apart from these two, there are other types on the market as well, each offering unique advantages for different applications. These reflective films are used in packaging to enhance visibility and attract attention to products on retail shelves.</p></p>
