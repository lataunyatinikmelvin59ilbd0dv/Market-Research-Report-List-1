<p><p>Neopentyl glycol is a key ingredient in the production of high-performance coatings, adhesives, and plastics. It is primarily produced using two main methods - Disproportionation Method and Hydrogenation Method. The Disproportionation method involves reacting formaldehyde with isobutyraldehyde, while the Hydrogenation method involves reacting dimethyl terephthalate with hydrogen gas. Both methods result in the production of neopentyl glycol with varying properties and applications, catering to different market needs and requirements.</p></p>
