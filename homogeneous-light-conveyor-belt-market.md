<p><p>Homogeneous light conveyor belt market includes two main types: calendering process and coating process. In the calendering process, the belt material is passed through a series of rollers to achieve the desired thickness and surface finish. On the other hand, in the coating process, a layer of material is applied onto a base belt using methods such as spraying or dipping. Both processes are used to produce high-quality conveyor belts that are durable, efficient, and suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
