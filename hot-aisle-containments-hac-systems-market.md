<p><strong>The Hot Aisle Containments (HAC) Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Aisle Containment</li><li>Vertical Aisle Containment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Horizontal Aisle Containment systems are designed to contain hot air in the aisle between server racks through the use of barriers and panels. This helps to efficiently direct hot air towards cooling systems, improving overall efficiency and reducing energy costs. </p><p>Vertical Aisle Containment systems, on the other hand, use overhead panels to create a barrier between the hot and cold aisles. This system is popular for its flexibility and ability to adapt to varying data center layouts, allowing for effective containment of hot air and improved cooling efficiency.</p></p>
