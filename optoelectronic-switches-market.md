<p><p>Optoelectronic switches come in different types based on their design and features. The Amplifier Separated Type includes a separate amplifier unit, providing flexibility in system configuration. The Built-In Amplifier Type integrates the amplifier within the switch body, offering a more compact solution. The Built-In Power Supply market includes switches with an integrated power supply, reducing the need for external power sources. Each type caters to different needs in terms of size, functionality, and ease of installation in optoelectronic systems.</p></p>
