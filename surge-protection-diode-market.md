<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Surge Protection Diode Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ESD (Electro Static Discharge)</li><li>TVS (Transient Voltage Suppression)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surge protection diode market consists of two main types - ESD (Electro Static Discharge) and TVS (Transient Voltage Suppression) diodes. ESD diodes are designed to protect electronic devices from damage due to static electricity. They provide a low breakdown voltage and high surge current capability. TVS diodes, on the other hand, are specifically designed to protect against transient voltage spikes or surges in electronic circuits. They have a higher breakdown voltage and are used to prevent damage from power surges or lightning strikes.</p></p>
