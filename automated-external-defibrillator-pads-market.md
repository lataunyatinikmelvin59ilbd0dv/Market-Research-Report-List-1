<p><p>Automated External Defibrillator (AED) Pads come in two main market types: Pediatric Defibrillator Pads and Adult Defibrillator Pads. Pediatric pads are designed specifically for children under the age of 8 or weighing less than 55 pounds, delivering a lower shock energy level to suit their needs. Adult pads, on the other hand, are meant for individuals over the age of 8 or weighing more than 55 pounds. Both types of pads are essential components of AED devices used in emergency situations to restore normal heart rhythm.</p></p>
