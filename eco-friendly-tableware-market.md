<p><p>The eco-friendly tableware market offers three main types of products: starch type, double degradation plastics type, and pulp type. Starch type tableware is made from biodegradable materials such as corn, potatoes, or wheat starch, ensuring it can easily break down harmlessly in the environment. Double degradation plastics type refers to tableware made from biodegradable and compostable plastics, which can degrade into smaller particles and eventually decompose completely. Pulp type tableware is manufactured using natural fibers like bamboo or sugarcane, making it environmentally friendly and easily degradable.</p></p>
