<p><p>Liquid phytases market can be categorised into two main types: Acid Phytase and Alkaline Phytase. Acid phytases are enzymes that work optimally in acidic conditions, while alkaline phytases function best in alkaline environments. Acid phytases are typically used in animal feed to improve phosphorus utilisation and reduce environmental pollution, while alkaline phytases are commonly employed in the food industry to enhance nutritional value and digestibility of plant-based proteins. Both types of phytases play a crucial role in various industries, contributing to improved efficiency and sustainability.</p></p>
